---
title: "Why I like building small AI tools"
date: "2025-12-21"
description: "A short note on leverage, automation, and compounding."
---

Most “AI” projects fail because they try to be big. I prefer small tools that:

- reduce a recurring cost (time, money, attention)
- are easy to maintain
- are measurable

That’s the same lens I use for FIRE and investing: build systems that compound.
